Meet the Founder

Hello! My name is Lindsay Jenkins, and I created Speak with Strength to help students and young professionals conquer their fear of public speaking.

As someone who once struggled to speak up in meetings or interviews, I know how overwhelming it can feel to find your voice. This platform is a safe space to practice, grow, and build the confidence you need to succeed, whether you're preparing for a presentation, a job interview, or just want to express yourself clearly.

Why I started Speak with Strength: I wanted to give people especially my son the tools I wish I had when I was starting out. We offer support,feedback,and a community that understands what it means to feel nervous but still show up and try anyway.
